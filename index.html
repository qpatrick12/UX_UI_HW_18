ABOUT

Hi! My name’s Quinn Blanchfield, and I’m a junior UX/UI designer! If you’ve arrived on this page you’re interested in learning more about me, and what inspires my design process. As a student of art & design from a young age, I’ve always had an appreciation for many creative endeavours, from different time periods and cultural backgrounds.

One of my favourite things about design I have come to appreciate is the balance between functionality and aesthetics, that when successfully struck, provides satisfying solutions to unique challenges. I believe the main goal of a good designer is to demonstrate an understanding of what makes something beautiful, while maintaining a certain degree of empathy for a perspective user.

As a creative person, I’m open to having new experiences and developing myself as a designer. I love a challenge, and find that learning new skills, and applying my knowledge to produce novel solutions, is the spark that ignites my creative process. I hope this comes across as you explore my portfolio!

Thanks, Quinn
